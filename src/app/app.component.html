Wiki-Randomizer is an application that extends Wikimedia's random article functionality, allowing you to specify a set of languages and wikisites to randomly choose from.

The app will first choose a random language, and then a random site, and finally retrieve a random article from that language/site combo.

If you do not select any languages and/or sites, your random articles will be chosen from all of the valid language/site combinations.
